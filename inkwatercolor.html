illustration - ink and watercolor

Materials I use when working with ink and watercolor:

  • Arches hot press watercolor paper
  • Deleter ink - Black #3
  • Tachikawa nibs - Maru, G-pen
  • Daniel Smith watercolors
  • Escoda Reserva sable brushes (or whatever, but i have two of those and they are so nice)

I generally buy art supplies from Jetpens.com, eBay, or Blick.

One of my favorite resources for learning about the more technical aspects of watercolor painting is handprint.com. The watercolor palette I use today I made using much of the advice in the "guide to watercolor pigments" section of the website.
