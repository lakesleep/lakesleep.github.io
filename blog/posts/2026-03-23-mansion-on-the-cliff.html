mansion on the cliff

Date: May 14, 2024 AST
Weather: 84°F Clear
Location: Baie Nettlé, Marigot, Saint Martin

# day 7

We dinghy out to a good snorkeling spot. found many treasures, many strange creatures I’ve never seen and cannot name. Cannot stop thinking about these trilobite-looking creatures in a secret beach inside a cliff.

We swam further, to a new beach.

Climbed up a cliff to an abandoned mansion. We snuck through a gateway in the cliff face that was held shut with a board painted in pink paint and the warning: “beware of dogs”.

Suddenly in giant 80s styled beach house mansion. Destroyed ceilings in some of the rooms. Us looking through everything. I find a little rusty lantern, not even big enough for a tea light. Tie it to my hip via swimsuit. Many many rooms. All expensively decorated in bad taste. We walk around back and hear dogs barking. Heart racing we have to get out

Walking back to door we hear them coming closer. L’s the last one to slip out, as soon as he does the dogs hit the door barking. Scrambling down the cliff rocks to the beach below. Running down the sand, wading into the water. We see a man on top of the cliff, filming us. We put on our snorkels and slip into the sea, swim away.

The last thing he records is us swimming into the sea.
