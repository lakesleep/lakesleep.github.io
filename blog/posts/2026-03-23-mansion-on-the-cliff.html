mansion on the cliff

Date: May 14, 2024
Weather: 84°F Clear
Location: Baie Nettlé, Marigot, Saint Martin

# day 7

We dinghy out to a good snorkeling spot. found many treasures, many strange creatures I’ve never seen and cannot name. Cannot stop thinking about these trilobite-looking creatures in a secret beach inside a cliff.

Leaving the dinghy behind, we swam further, to a new beach.

Climbed up a cliff to an abandoned mansion. We snuck through a gateway in the cliff face that was held shut with a plywood board painted in pink paint and the warning: “beware of dogs”.

Suddenly in giant 80s styled beach house mansion. Destroyed ceilings in some of the rooms. Us looking through everything. I find a little rusty lantern, not even big enough for a tea light. Tie it to my hip via swimsuit. Many many rooms. All expensively decorated in bad taste. We take our time. When we finally walk around back, we hear dogs barking. Distant, coming closer. Heart racing we have to get out

Rushing back to cliff face gateway. we hear them coming. L’s the last one to slip out and seal the gateway with the board. as soon as he does the dogs hit the plywood barking. It shakes as their bodies pound against it. We are scrambling down the cliff rocks to the beach below. We are running down the sand, wading into the water. A man on top of the cliff is filming us.

We put on our snorkels and slip into the sea, swim away.
